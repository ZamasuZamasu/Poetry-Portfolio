Inner Feelings

When things appear off track,
Life appears dull and broken,
Lots of reasons to cry,
Unable to find one to smile.

Aware that people around you care,
Everyone always try to cheer you,
Yet when dark clouds hover,
Everything seems out of my reach.

Not everyone hates,
Not everyone loves,
Hurts when you push your limits up,
Yet situations pull you down.

Aghast to see my inner self,
Complexities I can't understand,
How to tell those who care,
that I care when it mattered the most.

Yonder I never understand myself,
lest its too late,
Hope to see the sunshine,
While the end of my darkness awaits.
