Hope

Yeah time changes,
It goes from good to bad,
But it does effect the fate,
to make it perfectly good for you.

Things go bad to worse,
To trouble you for a while,
Makes you feel weaker, but turns you stronger,
To face the tomorrow.

Losing someone isn't easy to forget,
Hoping for good is,
Keep up the courage dear,
Everything's gonna be fine for you always.

She went away from you in body,
But always for you in soul,
Still holds your hand, when you fall,
And hugs you, when you cry.

You may not see her,
but she feels you, and your feelings,
She wants you to be that one,
Which she always dreamed for you.

If you cry, she would too,
If you smile, she would too,
If you win, she would be proud of you,
If you try, she would cheer you.

So dear, don't lose heart,
Let your heart beat for her,
Make your mind to honour her,
With all the good you do in life.
