Life Sick

Sick of listening to people
about your downfall
for every bad phase of life
feels like living in a dark hole.

Hurts to see when some people listen,
yet you unable to say what you mean,
Hurts to feel I love those people,
die for their care, yet remorseful from within.

Painful to see when people dismiss,
pain as childish, pain as common,
When all fingers aren't the same for all,
then how can miseries be the same for all.

Hurts to imagine all your dreams,
all your plans, shattering in front of you,
No matter how hard to plan,
there's something which always derails it.

Sick of relatives, sick of those,
who are our own, enemies from within,
who never care for you to live,
and if you do, then won't let you peaceful.

Unable to comprehend how things are going,
A feeling to die one day, then a feeling to recover,
can't see the end too soon, for I never,
want tears in those eyes who always care for me.
