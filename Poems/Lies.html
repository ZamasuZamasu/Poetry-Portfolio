Lies

Something which can bind us,
Something which can tear us apart,
If it's not for the right purpose,
It's always worse than one can ever thought.

Some say it out of habit,
Some say it to defend themselves,
It hurts the most when some lies,
Defend others, only to feel hurt later.

All the ideals move to trash,
When a difficult situation passes by,
No matter how much good one does,
It still backfires, worse than expected.

Mind boggles with trouble when,
Supportive people surround you,
Though they try their best to support you,
Yet you fall deep into darkness.

No matter how much you open up,
You still can't open up everything,
Every painful story not necessarily
Ends with a comic ending.

Some lies are good to be said,
In order to protect your peace of mind,
Hopefully things turn around,
Though I am not hopeful this time.
