How does death feel like?

How it feels?
To die from inside
A smile from outside
Trauma from all sides.

A grope of agony
A fear of present
Pain to begin with
And pain to end to.

It still seems fine
If things appear bright
But rotting from inside
Crying for help, which none can do.

Hopelessly hopeful,
Recklessly aghast,
Hope mind doesn't take decisions
That might end my life.

The one being a reason for others happiness,
Who knew will be drenched in a blood of sadness?
The one who deserved to live peacefully,
Who thought will be crippled to nothing?

There's no road, there's only heights,
Not to climb, but to drop from,
Why not dying for real,
When mind slowly takes you from within.
