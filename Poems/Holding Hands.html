Holding Hands

Long, long ago,
We crossed paths with each other,
And fell in love, and showered ourselves with affection,
Like no other.

We travelled through this journey,
Full of ups and downs,
Holding hands together,
Broke through all the obstacles that tried to break us.

"I care for you and you love me"
These words were our feelings
"When I fell down, you pushed me up"
That's how we inspired each other.

We stood the test of time,
Bad things came to move us apart,
But our love was too strong for it
To break us and move apart.

"You were my blessing, and my muse"
That's what we think about each other
"I think God blessed me with you"
Our thought say this to each other.

Love is a feeling, an emotion,
Empowered with support, care, happiness,
And we found these all in our lives,
The bond we shared, gave us all of it, to cherish.

From childhood friendship to young love,
From childish feelings, to "Love forever" memories,
Our love evolved,
We grew up, became aged, lost our outward beauty,
But what never changed, in fact,
Even grew more and more, is our love.

As we go through this amazing journey of life,
Let's put those sad feelings of adversities aside,
And look forward to Illumine our lives,
With forever cherishing and mesmerizing happiness.
