Intentions

Misunderstood for what you say,
Misunderstood for what you mean,
It wasn't the way you expected,
But happened otherwise, not likable.

Speaking loud, being open,
Seldom take a back seat,
When people lack something to criticize,
blame you unnecessarily.

Too many rights make you feel good,
A bit of wrong makes others the same,
You didn't seem wrong in your way,
Unless critics ruin your day.

Just some hope, just some light,
That's what makes people shine,
Demoralizing kills an individual,
robbing off its own shine.
