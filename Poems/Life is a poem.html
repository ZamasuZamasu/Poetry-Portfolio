Life is a Poem

Life is like a poem,
Thoughts are like words,
Flowing through our mind,
Brimming it with emotions,
Instilling in it curiosity,
To ask for something,
To know about it,
To feel and imagine it,
Creating our own dream world with it.

Decisions are like rhymes,
A righteous one can make life divine,
Just like a poem with lovely rhymes,
A wrong one, leads to a crime,
And we end up saying "It's, not fine".

Happy moments are like literary devices,
Enriching our heart,
Like the latter enriches the text.

Love is like alliteration,
May not make sense, but is surely fun to find,
Anywhere, any day,
In a prose, stanza or play.

Tragedies are a stanza's end,
Where the flow ends, and we cry for more,
Of what that lovely stanza told,
That's what life is,
Where we are supposed to live, love and stay safe.
