A "Sweet" Poem

She's a flowery persona,
Loves flowers, and is like one,
Cares for others, helps a lot,
A nice person to talk to.

An extrovert, loved by all,
Smart, sweet, and humble,
Intelligent with lots of ideas,
That makes her mind bumble.

She feels lonely, but doesn't show these,
While she helps others when they need,
She feels she's weird, strange taste of life,
Maybe that's the best way to live a life.

Thankful for a good thing she did,
For it helped me in a way I never guessed,
Grateful, in a way I can't explain,
Maybe these words can.
