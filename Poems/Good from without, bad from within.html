Good from without, bad from within

A smile from without,
But a cry from within,
I felt shattered with a question of,
How it happened to me?

Too much support from outside,
Yet I could avail none,
Had a couple of breakdowns,
But couldn't shed a tear in any one.

I reminisce, being alone,
Even though I wasn't,
Trying to scream, yell, suffocate,
But all in vain.

Thoughts of ending myself,
flashed through my mind,
But the guilt of making my dear ones sad,
Forbade me from this act.

Felt confused, agitated, aghast,
Beautiful dreams turned into nightmares,
Loving someone was a remedy,
But for me it turned to be a poison.

When I lost hope of everything,
Things started getting worse and worse,
Suddenly I crossed paths with someone,
And my situation turned.

I met the light,
which rekindled my spirits,
A few lucky moments of conversations,
Removed all the fright.

Things got better with time,
Thanks to that optimistic light,
I found back my rhythm, and broke throught my nightmares,
To dream peacefully, with a hope of getting better.

Once felt bad, now its getting fine,
Not cent percent, but still better than an year before,
But I believe I'll be the best soon,
And show what I am worth of.

Now I'm at present, chasing a dream,
All away from those midnight scream,
And that's how I end, with an advice
Don't hide your feelings, for you'll have 'tearless' cry.
