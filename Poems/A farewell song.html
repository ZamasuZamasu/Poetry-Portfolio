A Farewell Song

Its the time to leave,
With loads of memories,
Don't know how to control feelings (oh)..

Beautiful times flew away,
Wish to relive that again,
That hope never seems to end...

Yeah, Good byes,
To all of them we loved the most,
Yeah Good Byes,
To all of them we cared the most....

People go, but not memories,
And these all are our ecstasies,
Put aside all the bad that ever happened,
And enjoy the love we've got before....

Yeah, Good byes,
Thank them all who were with us
Yeah Good byes,
Cherish the thoughts they leave to us....

We end the time (yeah),
On a beautiful note...
We end our journey (We end our journey), Today,

Now life isn't gonna same,
But that feeling won't ever end..
Of love and care, and that merry childhood...
Yeah love and care, and that merry childhood...

Good byes to all!
Good luck to you,
Don't ever fall,
Let your problem fall in front of you!
